Test your memory!

In this online study you will be asked to complete two phases: one training phase and one testing phase. In the training phase you will be shown groups of geometric shapes. Your task will be to try and remember these groups of shapes. After seeing 24 groups, you can choose to move onto the testing phase or you can continue the learning trials if you think you need more time to memorise them. You will be tested on it later.

In the testing phase you will be shown either one or two shapes. You will need to add one shape to make a complete group.

You are free to withdraw at any time during the study, any completed tasks until the point of leaving will be deleted and destroyed. You are also free to withdraw your data at any time up until the point of anonymous publication of these data, which will occur at the time the journal article is be published. To withdraw during the experiment, simply close your browser tab.
